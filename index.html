Our Story

At Lumina, we believe in the power of light to transform spaces and create moments of comfort, relaxation, and joy. Our journey began five years ago in a small workshop with a simple passion for craftsmanship.

Every candle is handcrafted using sustainable materials, premium scents, and with meticulous attention to detail. We source our ingredients ethically and are committed to creating products that are kind to both you and the environment.
